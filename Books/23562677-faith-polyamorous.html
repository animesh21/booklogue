So where we left off with Hale, Taylor and Alder was the completion of the mating thrall and the departure of Hale. Taylor our main female lead is in love with both twin wolves Hale and Alder for the qualities that they are different, despite all her efforts to avoid loving them both she couldn't resist.

That being said the segway from Safety into Faith leads us through the navigation of the post mating pregnant Taylor adjustment period. As to keep from spoiling it for you, I will say that it's definitely shorter than safety so it turned out to be a lunchtime read for me, but packed quite a bit of drama into the short read.
